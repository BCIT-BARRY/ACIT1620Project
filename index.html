Welcome to BCIT Police Service

As a college police service, we focus on the public safety issues that are a priority for the students of BCIT and allocate resources to respond quickly to emerging trends. As a campus service, we are accountable to the students we serve through a police board, and we want to know what matters to you.

BCIT Police Service supports a diverse and growing campus community. Our policing model is founded in a culture of respectful collaboration and engagement. From Day 1, our goal is to be diverse and inclusive, and to reflect the community we serve.

How we serve BCIT

  • Visible patrols around campus buildings and parking lots
  • Support for students, staff, and visitors
  • Campus safety education and crime prevention tips
  • Partnerships with BCIT services and student groups
