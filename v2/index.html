Community policing for BCIT.

As a College police service, we focus on the public safety issues that are a priority for the students of BCIT and allocate resources to respond quickly to emerging trends. As a campus service, we are accountable to the students we serve through a police board and we want to know what matters to you.

BCIT Police Service supports one of Canada’s fastest-growing and most diverse cities – and we will grow to become one of the country’s largest independent police services. Our policing model is founded in a culture of respectful community collaboration and engagement. From Day 1, our organization has set a goal of being diverse and inclusive, and to reflect the community we serve.
