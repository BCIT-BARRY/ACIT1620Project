Recruitment & Application Process

The BCIT Police Service is looking for students who are committed to safety, teamwork, and community. Our recruitment process is designed to make sure applicants are prepared for the physical, academic, and ethical demands of campus policing.

Minimum Requirements

To be considered for a BCIT Police Recruit position, applicants should:

  • Be a registered BCIT student in good academic standing.
  • Be at least 18 years of age.
  • Be able to work evenings and weekends for campus events.
  • Demonstrate professionalism, integrity, and good judgement.
  • Be willing to complete training in communications and campus safety.

Application Steps

The application process is completed in several steps so we can get to know you and support you through training.

  1. Step 1 – Learn about the role
    Review this page and our About section to understand what BCIT Police officers do on campus. Make sure the role fits your goals, schedule, and comfort level.

  2. Step 2 – Submit your application
    Complete the Recruit Application Form (available from our Contact page) and include your name, BCIT student number, program, and a short statement about why you want to join.

  3. Step 3 – Interview
    Shortlisted applicants will be invited to a panel interview with members of the BCIT Police Service. We will ask about your experiences, decision-making, and how you handle stressful situations.

  4. Step 4 – Reference & background check
    We may contact one or two references (such as an instructor, employer, or supervisor) to confirm your reliability and professionalism.

  5. Step 5 – Training & onboarding
    Successful applicants will receive basic training in radio use, campus safety procedures, note-taking, and community engagement. You will be assigned a mentor officer for your first shifts.

  6. Step 6 – Active duty on campus
    After training, you will begin working patrols and assisting at events with experienced members of the BCIT Police Service. Ongoing coaching and feedback will help you grow in the role.

Why Join BCIT Police?

  • Gain real experience in communication, teamwork, and leadership.
  • Build skills that transfer to law enforcement, security, and public safety careers.
  • Be part of a visible, positive presence on campus.
